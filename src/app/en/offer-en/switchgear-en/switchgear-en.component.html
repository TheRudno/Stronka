Switchgears (VN and NN)


Please feel invited to acquaint yourself with our offer concerning low-voltage and medium voltage switchgears. They come from prestigious and prised manufacturers. The medium-voltage switchgears are offered in air insulation and in SF6.

The first one is a cheaper solution which enables any configuration of fields.

The second one – SF6 insulation – characterizes itself by a compact construction and low failure rate.

We offer full cables accessories, transformers and post-warranty service for medium-voltage switchgears.

As for low-voltage switchgears there is a possibility to order it on a frame or in a cabinet, according to individual needs.

The switchgears are equipped in a disconnector or main switch and a specific number of outflows with the possibility of reserve places - according to the client’s needs .

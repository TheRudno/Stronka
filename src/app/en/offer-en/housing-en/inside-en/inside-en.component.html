Transformer stations with internal service


Containers of the stations with internal service are made of reinforced concrete in the form of a monolithic cast of a cellar and a roof of a building. In case of the buildings with internal service there is a possibility to combine them into a set of buildings, e.g. for the need of main power supply points.

They are designed for installation of one, two or more transformers depending on the individual needs of the client.

They ensure ideal working conditions for wiremen and people servicing the stations as well as quickness and easiness of installation at the destination place. There is also possibility to create a station with even three walls of fireproofing which enables setting the station near other buildings or on the plot line.

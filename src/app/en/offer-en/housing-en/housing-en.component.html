Transformer stations containers


We create transformer stations containers made of reinforced concrete. They are conducted according to individual needs of the client. We provide consultancy starting from the concept, through design up to realisation. The containers meet all requirements in the field of suitability for use in energetics, safety and environment protection. We use solutions of the leading manufacturers of transmission and distribution systems for switch rooms. There is also a possibility of picking the container into the device and delivery of a complete transformer station to the place of destination. The containers are produced mainly in two types of usage, plus special containers:

- containers with external service, type ARMES-Z
- containers with internal service, type ARMES-W
- special containers ARMES-S

We design and execute containers with fireproof walls, underground, tower, column and bunker stations containers on special order.

Transformer stations with external service


Containers of the stations with external service are made of reinforced concrete in the form of a monolithic cast. The container consists of two elements. They are designed for installation of one or two transformers depending on individual needs of the client. We also provide solutions which enable to install the medium-voltage switchgear in air insulation in these buildings.

Their compact design ensures quick and easy installation at the destination place. There is also possibility to create a station with even three walls of fireproofing which enables setting the station near other buildings or on the plot line.

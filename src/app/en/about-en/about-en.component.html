Armes Company has been providing services in the electrical segment for over 10 years.

We offer delivery of concrete container transformer stations as well as fully-equipped stations. We also sell transmission and distribution systems of low-voltage and medium-voltage switchgears, oil-type and dry-type transformers (new or regenerated). We offer complete solutions in the field of sealings from Hauff-Technik GmbH & Co. KG which we also use in our containers.

The company also deals with execution of electrical projects and consultancy in the field of construction and selection of concrete container transformer stations. We provide short-term and long-term hire of transformer stations.

We operate on Polish and foreign markets which cover mainly the Czech Republic and Slovakia. We are in possession of all crucial certificates and approvals for the use of our containers.
